03How this was built

Researched, ideated, and adversarially pressure-tested.

Grounded

Hermes capabilities pulled from its live docs; luxury-agency operations and the AI-tooling landscape researched from the field — so the ideas are real, not generic.

Ideated & ranked

Opportunities generated across four value lenses, then deduped, scored on impact × effort, and sequenced by blast radius into a phased roadmap.

Pressure-tested

A 20-year-agency-owner + CISO critic killed the overhyped, flagged every boundary leak, and forced the ownership model — so what survived is what holds.

Every figure here is modeled from industry benchmarks, not measured from a specific agency's books. Before committing budget, instrument real baselines — trips/yr, blended commission, commission-leakage rate, admin hours, conversion — and re-run the ROI model on your own numbers.
